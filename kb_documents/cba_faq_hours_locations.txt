# Test Business Hours and Location Specifics

## Standard Business Hours
* Weekdays: Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
* Weekends: Closed on Saturdays and Sundays. We believe in giving our team members quality time to spend with their families.
* Holiday Closures: Shops are closed on major national holidays. Check with your local branch for holiday schedule updates.

## Franchise Locations and Contact Info
* Franchise Model: Each Test shop is independently owned and operated by local members of the community.
* Finding a Local Shop: You can easily find contact details, phone numbers, and street addresses for any of our over 250 locations nationwide on the official website locator.
* Scheduling Appointments: Bookings can be made online, over the phone, or directly through the voice assistant configuration portal.
