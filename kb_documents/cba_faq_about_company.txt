# Test Comprehensive Company and Services FAQ

## Section 1: About the Company & Policies

### Q: What is Test?
Test has been providing reliable, high-quality automotive maintenance and repair services for more than 40 years. Our business model is built on transparency, honesty, and education, ensuring that customers understand exactly how their vehicle will be repaired without any unnecessary upsells.

### Q: Are Test shops independently owned?
Yes, each location is a franchise that is independently owned and operated by local members of their community. This allows each shop to provide personalized, friendly service backed by a trusted national brand.

### Q: What is the Nice Difference®?
The Nice Difference® is our signature commitment to changing the way people feel about auto service. It means we provide:
- Transparent, honest service where you stay in control of your repairs.
- Clean, comfortable waiting rooms.
- Access to free Wi-Fi and amenities.
- A complimentary local shuttle service to take you home, to work, or to school and pick you up when your car is ready.

### Q: What warranty do you offer on repairs?
We stand behind our work with our standard Nice Difference® Warranty. This covers eligible parts and labor for 3 years or 36,000 miles, whichever term lasts longer (providing maximum benefit to the customer). The warranty is honored nationwide at any of our over 250 locations across the United States.

### Q: Do you accept extended warranties?
Yes, we work with all major extended warranty providers. Customers are encouraged to discuss their extended warranty carrier with their service advisor when booking an appointment to confirm coverage details.

### Q: Do you perform state vehicle inspections?
Yes. We offer courtesy inspections to all customers, and where required by state law, our locations perform official state safety and emissions inspections, completing all necessary documentation and legal paperwork.

## Section 3: Nice Difference Care+ (Subscription & Vehicle Service Contracts)

### Q: What is Nice Difference Care+?
Nice Difference Care+ is a subscription-based vehicle service contract (also known as an extended warranty) offered by Test. It is designed to provide comprehensive, long-term coverage for your vehicle to make ownership easier, nicer, and worry-free.

### Q: Who administers the Nice Difference Care+ plans?
While branded by Test, Care+ plans are provided by Northcoast Warranty Services, Inc. and administered by Test Warranty Admin, LLC.

### Q: How does Care+ differ from the standard Nice Difference Warranty?
- **Nice Difference Warranty:** This is a standard limited warranty automatically provided for free on any eligible repair work done at our shops (covering parts and labor for 3 years or 36,000 miles).
- **Nice Difference Care+:** This is a paid, subscription-based extended service contract that covers a much broader scope of mechanical breakdowns and components over a longer term, beyond individual repairs.

### Q: Does Care+ require an initial vehicle inspection?
Yes. To enroll in Care+, your vehicle typically requires an initial inspection at a Test location to identify and exclude pre-existing conditions from coverage.

### Q: Is there a deductible for Care+ repairs?
Yes, deductibles apply based on your specific plan (typically around $400 for repairs done at non-Test locations). However, active-duty military, veterans, first responders, and repairs performed directly at Test locations may qualify for a $0 deductible.

### Q: Do repairs under Care+ require authorization?
Yes. All repairs covered under the Care+ vehicle service contract require pre-authorization from the Test Warranty Admin team before work begins.
