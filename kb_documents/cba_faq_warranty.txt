# Test Warranty Policy
Test stands behind our work with our standard Nice Difference® Warranty.

## Nationwide Parts and Labor Warranty
* Coverage Term: Covers eligible parts and labor for 3 years or 36,000 miles, whichever occurs last.
* Nationwide Acceptance: The warranty is honored at any of the over 250 Test locations across the United States. If you experience an issue while traveling or after moving, you can visit any local franchise.
* Coverage Scope: Applies to most standard repairs and replacement parts. Excludes wear-and-tear items or repairs specifically noted otherwise on your invoice.
* Transferability: The warranty remains tied to the vehicle, giving subsequent owners peace of mind.
